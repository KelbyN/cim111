All about the chef

My name is Kelby Norfleet, but sometimes I like to call myself Chef Kelboyardee. I know it sounds ridiculous, but there is a ring to it. Ever since I was young , I loved to cook. I was always in the kitchn with my parents, and they always joke that I was the first at the table for dinner. And when my mom had to work late, my siblings and I would be responsible for getting dinner reaady. I love to experiment, making new things that I see or eat at a restauraunt. I also love cooking food that is vegan. I find that most people or even restaurants suck at making flavorful healthy vegen food. I however am very good at it. Food is my passion.
