All About The Chef

My name is kelby Norfleet, but i sometimes enjoy calling myself, Chef Kelboyardee. It sounds silly, but theres a ring to it. There are a few things I love most in life, and that eating, and cooking. i started cooking young, in the kitchen with my parents, or cooking when my mother had to work late. I love to experiment and cook cuisines from different places, or trying to replicate something i ate at restaurant

I also love to cook non conventional food, such as vegan food. But really good vegan food. One of my favorite things to make is cheesecake. I even started selling them about a year ago.
