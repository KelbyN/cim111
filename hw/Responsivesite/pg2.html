I hope that one day I can travel India, and discover its culture through its food!

My mother's family is from Louisianna, so I love good creole, cajun, southern food. It's packed with flavor, and never disappoints a crowd.

One of my favorite things about asian food are the flavorful sauces. I love making them, and storing them in my fridge for a variety of dishes.
