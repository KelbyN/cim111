Classic Cheesecake

This delicioius cheesecake serves around 14 people, depending on how big you slice it.

Ingredients:

  • 2 packs Graham Crackers
  • 2 tbsp Butter
  • 24 ounces Cream Cheese
  • 16 ounces sour cream
  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 5tsp vanilla extract
  • 5 eggs
  • juice of half a lemon

Directions

For the crust:

1. Prepare the pan by lining it with parchment paper. Set aside

2. Combine the butter and crushed graham crackers in a bowl together. Mix well.

3. Press mixture into a pan and bake at 350 degrees for about 20 minutes

For the Cheesecake mix

4. Beat the room temperature cream cheese until soft, and fluffy.

5. Add in the sugar about a third cup at a time, stopping to beat in the sugar completely before adding more.

6. beat in the eggs, one at a time

7. Add in the lemon juice, the vanilla extract. Sift in the flour. Stir well to combine, and make sure there are no lumps. Then add in the sour cream.

8. Pour mixture into the pan you prepared for the crust, and bake in the oven for about 80 minutes, or until the top barely jiggles when touched

9. Carefully remove cheesecake from pan by flipping it over onto a flat surface, and then flipping it onto your serving dish. If necessary, run a knife around the edges and soak the bottim of the pan in hot water before trying to remove. Serve with any topping you like!

**See tip**

If the cheesecake is tight fitting in the pan, you can use a springform pan instead. But you must wrap it well in foil. Or, simple run a knife around the inside of the pan, and allow it to sit in hot water for about 5 minutes before removing it.
